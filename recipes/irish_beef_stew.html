Irish Stew

Comforting Irish Stew

This particular stew has all of the classic trimmings of a good Irish stew—meat, stock, plenty of root vegetables—with the addition of some Guinness extra stout, for its malty flavor and some Irish authenticity.

stews can contain potatoes, root vegetables, flavorings like beer or wine and herbs, and thickened with potato, flour, cornstarch, or nothing at all.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes, beef, thyme, tomato paste, carrots, black pepper, parsley, beef stock, salt, onion

Steps

  1. Brown the beef
  2. Add garlic and other stock ingredients, then simmer
  3. Add vegetables to the beef stew
  4. boil for 4 hours
  5. Grab a bowl and enjoy with a dash of parsley
